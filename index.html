BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a simple yet valuable tool that allows individuals to assess their overall body weight in relation to their height. By inputting height and weight data, users can quickly determine whether their weight falls within a healthy range or if they may be underweight, overweight, or within the ideal weight range for their height.
